Formal Systems in Biology - A resource

Browsing through my daily feed, I stumbled upon this neat resource which lists (and links to) fundamental ideas that have shaped the field of systems biology. To me, many of these publications represent some of the earliest attempts to reduce complex biological systems and processes into something simpler, more comprehensible, by viewing them through the lense of mathematics; truly groundbreaking efforts that paved the way for the current trends of ML/AI in biology. I'd strongly recommend anyone with an interest in the field to have a look at it; something that contains work from both Alan Turing and John von Neumann can't be anything but good!
