Formal Systems in Biology - A resource

Browsing thorugh my daily feed, I stumbled upon this neat resource which lists (and links to) some of the fundamental works that has been essential in forming the field of systems biology. To me, many of these publications represent some of the earliest attempts to reduce complex biological systems and processes into something simpler and more comprehensible, by viewing them through the eye of a mathematician; truly groundbreaking efforts. I'd strongly recommend anyone with an interest in the field to have a look at it - I mean - something that contains work from both Alan Turing and John von Neumann can't be anything but good!
