Alma Andersson

Computational Biologist

Royal Institute of Technology (KTH), Science For Life Laboratory (SciLifeLab)

Biography

Hello world, I’m Alma, a computational biologist who loves to explore ways of describing biological systems and their processes by using models based on statistical inference and machine learning. My work is usually applied to problems relating to single cell or spatial transcriptomics data, but I’m excited about computational methods in general, especially generative probabilistic models. Science is awesome and only comes second to my big passion in life, running. Also, Linux is my jam.
